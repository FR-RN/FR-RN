Open Science Journal Club in Bordeaux

Jan 19, 2022

The Bordeaux Neurocampus Graduate Program brings for 2022 a new activity to the Bordeaux Neurocampus community, the ReproducibiliTea Open Science Journal Club. ReproducibiliTea consists in a cycle of monthly meetings in which students and young researchers can join to discuss various topics related to Open Science around a warm cup of tea and (why not?) delicious cookies (as soon as the pandemic situation allows gatherings again).
