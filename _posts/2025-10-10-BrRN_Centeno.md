Le 10 Octobre 2025, Eduarda Centeno a animé un wébinaire sur **The Brazilian Reproducibility Network: past, present, and future.**.

 **Eduarda Centeno**, co-Executive Director, Brazilian Reproducibility Network – Brésil.


*Résumé* :
 In this presentation, Eduarda Centeno, co-Executive Director of the Brazilian Reproducibility Network (BrRN), will present an overview of the network—an initiative established to advance transparency, rigour, and reproducibility in scientific research across Brazil. She will trace BrRN’s origins and rapid expansion over its initial two years, highlighting key milestones, organizational structure, and the collaborative efforts that have shaped its development. The presentation will explore the network’s work across four strategic axes: community building, political advocacy, metascience, and educational training and resources. It will also bring the broader challenges and opportunities in cultivating a culture of reproducibility in Brazil and outline the network's future directions.
