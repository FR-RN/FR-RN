Le 12 décembre 2025, Emmanuel Boakye a animé un wébinaire sur **The African Reproducibility Network: Building Grassroots Capacity in Africa**.

**Emmanuel Boakye**, Executive Director & Founder, African Reproducibility Network – AREN.

*Résumé* :
In this presentation, Emmanuel Boakye, Executive Director of the African Reproducibility Network (AREN), will present on AREN's Local Network Leads training initiative and its role in establishing Local Networks in 28 institutions across 15 countries in Africa that are led by well-trained local leads. He will also share insights into how AREN has grown over the years and the efforts the AREN community is putting in to bridge the gaps in promoting open and reproducible research practices in Africa.

L'enregistrement du wébinaire est à retrouver sur [ce lien](https://www.canal-u.tv/chaines/rfrr/meet-the-networks-africa-emmanuel-boakye).
